Pasta Recipe

Defintions list:

al dente
- A correct degree of doneness for pasta and vegetables
baste
- Brush oil around item to be cooked to prevent dryness
pinenut
- Also called pignoli
  • The ingredients:
    100 g. flour
    10 g. sugar
    1 cup water
    2 eggs
    salt, pepper
  • The procedure:
    Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
    Pour in wet ingredients.
    Mix for 10 minutes.
    Bake for one hour at 300 degrees.
